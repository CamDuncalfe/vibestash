## Product Hunt

### Tagline Options
1. "the weird stuff people ship at 3am"
2. "a directory of vibe-coded apps and experiments"
3. "312 weird apps built with AI, vibes, and no business plan"

### Description (Draft)
VibeStash is a curated directory of the best vibe-coded products on the internet. The weird tools, funny experiments, and surprisingly useful things people build with Cursor, Claude, Bolt, and whatever drops next week.

Every product has a video demo. Browse by category (weird, games, creative, tools), upvote your favorites, and submit your own projects.

We're not a startup directory. We're the place you come when you want to see what unhinged thing someone shipped at 3am on a Tuesday.
